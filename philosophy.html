Our Philosophy

MappsLab has been created and is being developed
  • to be real classroom and real teacher friendly
  • to provide an instant example of joyful learning
  • to empower teachers to take back control of their classrooms and unite as professionals to become the primary agents for educational reform

An engaged classroom is a happy classroom. MappsLab reconnects teachers and students with their best learning selves, and nothing is more fun than learning.

Students move from passive collectors to active creators of their own narratives, turning them into readers, writers, and mathematicians. MappsLab is designed to be used across the curriculum - a compelling tool to tell stories and make learning relevant.

When students learn efficiently, they improve their test scores without sacrificing valuable learning and playtime. A teacher whose class tests well is freed of pressure to “teach to the test,” reasserting her role as a learning professional without administrative micro-managing.

A teacher with parental respect and support have the power to involve parents positively. Student products demonstrate what students know and the skills they’ve acquired, and can be shared effectively with parents and administrators.

Teachers in a social network of learning professionals are empowered to make needed system change from the grassroots up. MappsLab connects teachers across boundaries, providing opportunities for student collaboration and an antidote for teacher loneliness.
